Domain 1: Acceptability & Culture

1.1 Will using the App be likely to maintain or increase good health-related behaviours for the expected users irrespective of their cultural identity?

1.2 Is the purpose of the App clear and aligned in a positive manner to the intended userâ€™s health maintenance or improvement, whether directly (end user) or indirectly (via a clinician or service provider using the App on behalf of a user)?

1.3 Does the App make a claim about being intended for, or likely to have significant aspects of cultural sensitivity for Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples, specific CALD or CARM communities, LGBTQIA+ people or specific demographic groups? And / or does the App make a claim about taking a trauma-informed approach, being disability affirming or relevant for a specific group or community of people with disabilities such as neurodivergent people?

1.4 Does the app cater for the needs of users who live in regional, rural or remote areas of Australia?

Domain 2: Safety & Trustworthiness

2.1 Are restrictions/contraindications/limits/risks clearly communicated?

2.2 Evidence of conforming to safety-by-design principles?

2.3 Evidence for specific health claims/benefits from authoritative sources?

2.4 Is health information provided current?

2.5 Evidence of compliance with ethical codes (if not part of formal ethics review)?

2.6 Does the App claim safe operation (support channels, breach process, integration conformance)?

2.7 Are all costs clearly identified?

2.8 Is authorship/affiliations/interests/IP transparently disclosed?

2.9 Is advertising/sponsorship presence and source appropriately disclosed?

Domain 3: Usability & Equity

3.1 (WebApps) Does it support WCAG 2.1 AA? (General) Accessibility designed/supported?

3.2 Was it designed/tested with intended target users?

3.3 Was it designed with needs of low digital literacy/disability access in mind?

3.4 Are instructions clear, easy to understand? Alerts for out-of-range/errors?

Domain 4: Privacy & Security

4.1 Does it comply with relevant AU privacy policies (storage, location)?

4.2 Is there clear info on how user-derived data is used (algorithms, decisions)?

4.3 Does App provide user control over personal data (access, view, download, understand, erase)?

4.4 Is explicit consent obtained before collecting/using personal data beyond primary intent (e.g. AI training)?

4.5 Does App/website disclose third parties, how they use data, termination, retention/destruction?

4.6 Does App adhere to secure storage/exchange/messaging standards? Strong encryption? Conformance for health record access?

4.7 (If required) Does App implement secure authentication (MFA, biometrics etc)?

Domain 5: Technical & Quality

5.1 Is App in supported state (beyond beta)? Compliant with ISO/TS 82304-2:2021 or similar?

5.2 Is App technically reliable during usage on wide range of platforms?

5.3 (If AI/ML used) Has it been examined for biases/training errors?

5.4 (If AI/ML used) Is it compliant with consumer laws, reviewed for unethical behaviours?

5.5 Is App available across different devices/OS/screen sizes for expected community?

5.6 Does App provide user with technical support?

5.7 Is there comprehensive/clear documentation for users/developers?
